This audit evaluates Phantom Brand Co's search and AI visibility across seven areas — from technical infrastructure through to how frequently the brand appears in AI-generated responses.

Technical & UX — Crawlability, indexation, site speed, Core Web Vitals, and mobile usability. The health of the infrastructure search engines depend on.

On-Site SEO & Off-Site — Title tags, content quality, internal linking, backlink profile, and the on- and off-page signals that determine how individual pages rank.

Tools & Configuration — Analytics setup, Search Console accuracy, and the measurement infrastructure needed to act on performance data.

GEO / AI Visibility — How and how often the brand appears in AI Overviews, large language model citations, and generative search across major platforms.

Schema — Structured data markup that enables rich results, knowledge panel features, and machine-readable content signals.

Content Expansion
Thin content across most pages is the primary reason for low AI citation and weak keyword rankings.
Keyword Strategy
No keyword targeting on key pages. Without a defined strategy, content cannot rank for relevant search terms.
Organization Schema
No Organization schema on the homepage. Search engines and AI tools cannot verify brand identity or social profiles.
TL;DR Summaries
No summary intros on any page. Adding short TL;DR paragraphs is the fastest lever to earn AI citations.
llms.txt
File generated and ready to deploy. Tells AI crawlers what to index — zero development work required.
Secure Admin
Admin login is at the default URL. Any automated bot can target the login page — a quick config change to fix.
OpenGraph Tags
No OG tags sitewide. Every social share renders with no image, title, or description.
