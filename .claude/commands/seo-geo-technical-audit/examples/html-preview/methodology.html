How This Audit Works

March 2026 · Phantom Brand Co

This audit examines Phantom Brand Co's search and AI visibility across seven areas — from the technical infrastructure that search engines rely on to crawl the site, through to how frequently and accurately the brand appears in AI-generated responses.

Each finding is assessed against current best practice, weighted by its real-world impact, and prioritised so that the most valuable fixes are clearly visible without requiring a background in SEO. Recommendations are sequenced by urgency — the most impactful changes appear at the top of each section.

How We Score

Each element is rated on a five-point scale, from Not Present to Excellent, then multiplied by a weight that reflects its real-world impact on search and AI visibility. The result is a Priority Score — lower scores indicate higher urgency, so the most critical issues rise to the top of each section automatically.

Rating Scale

RatingValueWhat it means
Not Present1The element does not exist on the site.
Weak2Exists but implemented very poorly — significant issues present.
Medium3Functional, but with significant room to improve.
High4Mostly good — minor improvements possible.
Excellent5Best practice met — virtually no issues.
