> "We believe it's only possible to be truly healthy if you look after your gut every day. We commit to supporting **your gut health**."

**Brand Values:** Inclusive | Science-driven | Enjoyment
**Brand Personality:** Approachable | Knowledgeable | Full of life
**Official Tagline:** Supports gut health* *(always with asterisk)*

**Category Truth:** Our gut can impact many other aspects of our well-being, from digestive health to immunity to mood. Yogurt is a convenient, tasty, and effective way to support gut health.
**Product Truth:** Not all yogurts are equal. Besides having a naturally delicious taste, Activia also has a unique mix of cultures and billions of probiotics that reach the gut alive.
**Human Tension:** In a world where many of us are putting a lot of effort into improving our health through the food we eat, it's frustrating that I rarely feel healthy enough to make the most of my day.

## **4. Editorial Tone & Style**

**Tone to use:** Empowering | Optimistic | Science-based | Trustworthy | Purpose-driven | Consumer-centric

**Personality in practice:** Approachable and full of life — not clinical, not preachy. The brand knows its science but wears it lightly. Acknowledge the human tension rather than lecturing about it.

**Tone to avoid:**
- Overly promotional or clinical
- Moralizing, fear-based, preachy messaging
- Never tell consumers they "should" eat for gut health
- No exaggerated superlatives
- Do not imply medical treatment or cures

**Core educational pillar to reinforce:** Not all yogurts contain probiotics — Activia® does.

## **8. Content Structure Patterns**

### Product Page Structure
1. **Category tag** (above H1) — e.g., `PROBIOTIC CUPS`, `PROBIOTIC DRINKS`
2. **H1** — product name (e.g., "Peach Probiotic Low Fat Yogurt")
3. **Intro paragraph** — 2–3 sentences; flavor + probiotics + low fat; conversational, upbeat
4. **Pack size label** — `SINGLE` or `4` depending on SKU
5. **Feature bullets** (bold heading + 1-line description; 3–5 bullets):
   - **On-The-Go** — "Skip the spoon. Just shake and sip." (drinks only)
   - **Low fat** — "Made with cultured Grade A reduced fat milk."
   - **Real [Fruit]** — describe real fruit ingredient
   - **Creamy texture** — "A [dreamy/velvety] texture that keeps you [scooping/sipping]."
   - **Supports gut health** — "Billions of probiotics and active cultures in every [cup/bottle]."
6. **Nutrition Facts** (heading only — CMS populates)
7. **Ingredients** (heading only — CMS populates)
8. **FAQ section** — heading: "Got Questions About Our [Product]?" or "Frequently Asked Questions About Our [Product]:"
   - Always exactly **3 questions** per product page
   - Topics: probiotics/cultures strain, sugar content, storage/refrigeration, gluten-free status, calorie count; for flavor pages include a taste question
9. **Boilerplate footer** — "Good health starts with gut health" ⚠️ **Being phased out.** Currently crossed out on briefs. Do not include in new briefs. Replace with 3 product-specific FAQs.

### Drink vs. Cup Content Distinctions
| Drinks | Cups |
|---|---|
| "yogurt drink", "drinkable yogurt" | "yogurt cup", "spoonful" |
| "shake and sip", "on-the-go" | "grab-and-go", "breakfast or snack" |
| Feature: On-The-Go bullet | Feature: Sweetness/Real Fruit bullet |

### Blog/Educational Article Structure
- Education-first introduction | Simple science explanation | Practical daily routine integration
- Clear, digestible formatting | Action-oriented but not promotional | Myth-busting angle
