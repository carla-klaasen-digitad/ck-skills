## **6. Editorial Tone & Vocabulary**

**Tone:** Professionally warm · credibility-led (institutional trust signals first) · operator-focused (business outcomes not taste) · solutions-oriented · specific and factual — avoid consumer-facing language, vague sustainability claims, promotional hype

**Preferred Terms:** operator/operators (not "customers" or "clients") · foodservice / foodservice solution · portfolio · channel (healthcare channel, education channel, etc.) · B Corp certified / Certified B Corporation™ (Danone North America level only) · sustainable sourcing · plant-based (Silk, So Delicious) · dairy-free (So Delicious) · on-trend / menu trends

**Banned Words:** healthy (as product claim) · the best / #1 · natural · guilt-free · probiotic (use "live & active cultures") · consumer-facing superlatives

**Channel pages:** SEO H1 = operator segment + foodservice category · lead with operational challenge for that channel · highlight relevant brands/products · B Corp/sustainability messaging where relevant to procurement · CTA: Contact a rep / Request samples / Explore portfolio
