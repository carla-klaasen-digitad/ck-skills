## **1. Brand Overview**

**Brand:** REMIX Yogurt (United States) | **Parent:** Danone North America (Certified B Corporation™)

REMIX is not a standalone yogurt brand — it is a shared dual-compartment format (yogurt + separate mix-in toppings compartment) applied across three Danone parent brands with distinct nutritional platforms.

**The Three REMIX Sub-Lines:**

| Sub-Line | Base Brand | Nutritional Hook | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light + Fit® REMIX | Light + Fit | Up to 120 calories, fat free greek | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |
| Oikos® REMIX | Oikos | 11g protein, nonfat greek | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |
| Too Good & Co.® REMIX | Too Good & Co. | 60% less sugar, cultured ultra-filtered milk | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |

**Core Products & Flavors:**

*Light + Fit REMIX (Fat Free Greek Yogurt with Mix-Ins):*
- Strawberry Cheesecake (graham cookies, caramel pearls, dark chocolate)
- Brownie Sundae (brownie crumble, honey praline almond, butter toffee)
- Caramel Apple Snickerdoodle Pie (snickerdoodle pieces, white fudge chunks, cinnamon sprinkles)
- Caramel Apple Pie

*Oikos REMIX (Nonfat Greek Yogurt with Mix-Ins):*
- Cookies and Cream (cocoa cookie crumble, white fudge chunks)
- S'mores
- Salted Caramel
- Berry Dark Chocolate Crisp

*Too Good & Co. REMIX (Cultured Ultra-Filtered Milk with Mix-Ins):*
- Chocolate Covered Strawberry (chia granola, roasted almonds, chocolate pieces)
- Dark Chocolate Cherry Tart
- Peach Cinnamon Crisp

**Mission:** The fun of mixing meets the smarts of better-for-you yogurt — indulgent flavors and textures you stir in yourself.

## **7. Editorial Tone**

**Use:** Playful and energetic; snack-culture native — short punchy sentences, food-excitement language; casual and social-media adjacent; fun without being juvenile (skews 18–35); flavor-forward and sensory; confident about the better-for-you angle without over-explaining.

**Avoid:** Clinical or nutritional-report style; health-lecturing or virtue-signaling; exaggerated superlatives ("the best", "#1"); guilt-based messaging; long-form editorializing.

## **8. Vocabulary**

**Preferred:**
- Mix-ins / mix in (core format descriptor)
- Toppings / stir / dig in (action language)
- Craveable
- Bold / Snack strong (Oikos sub-line)
- Low calorie / up to 120 calories (L+F — factual only)
- 11g protein (Oikos — factual only)
- 60% less sugar (Too Good — vs. average yogurt with mix-ins; factual only)
- No artificial sweeteners (Too Good REMIX only)
- Live and active cultures
- Good source of calcium / protein (when nutritionally accurate)
- Fat free (L+F REMIX — factual only)

**Never Use:**
- Healthy (as a product claim)
- The best / #1
- Natural (use specific ingredient descriptions)
- Guilt-free
- Probiotic (use "live and active cultures")
- Sugar-free (use "60% less sugar" or "no added sugar" per approved claim)
- Clean label / Wholesome / Nutritious

⚠️ L+F REMIX contains sucralose and acesulfame potassium — NEVER claim "no artificial sweeteners" for L+F sub-line. This claim applies only to Too Good & Co. REMIX.

## **11. Content Structure Requirements**

### Product Pages
- SEO-optimized H1: sub-line + flavor name (e.g., "Oikos REMIX Cookies and Cream Greek Yogurt")
- Intro: 2–3 sentences; lead with mix-in experience and flavor, then nutritional hook
- Feature bullets (3–5): mix-in description (name toppings specifically), nutritional hook ("11g Protein" / "Up to 120 Calories" / "60% Less Sugar"), Fat Free (L+F only) or Nonfat (Oikos), Live and Active Cultures, No Artificial Sweeteners (Too Good REMIX only)
- FAQ: exactly **3 questions** — cover allergens, sweetener type (especially L+F vs. Too Good), mix-in ingredients, serving and storage

### Category / Landing Pages
- Lead with the mix-in format concept — stir it yourself, your snack your way
- Organize by sub-line with nutritional hook for each
- Include all active SKUs per sub-line
- Include "Where to Buy" CTA

### Recipe / Inspiration Content
- Lead with snack occasion or bowl build
- Highlight how REMIX mix-ins work as a base or component
- Keep fun and visual — yogurt bowl builds, snack boards, after-school ideas
- Tag by sub-line where relevant (protein bowl → Oikos REMIX; low-cal bowl → L+F REMIX)
